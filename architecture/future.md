## Long-Term Direction

Future architecture work should focus on making the foundation easier to adopt without weakening the core boundaries.
That includes clearer contracts, stronger compatibility rules, and better documentation for integration authors.

## What Should Remain Stable

- Engine agnosticism.
- The separation between core and adapters.
- ADR-driven structural change management.
- Documentation quality as a first-class concern.
