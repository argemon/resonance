## Our Standards

We expect contributors to act with respect, patience, and professionalism.
Healthy collaboration includes constructive feedback, good-faith interpretation, and a willingness to learn.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Deliberate sabotage, trolling, or abusive language.
- Repeatedly ignoring project boundaries or maintainer guidance.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces, issue trackers, pull requests, and all official communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, restrict, or reject participation when behavior undermines the safety or productivity of the community.
Severe concerns may be handled through private maintainer channels.
