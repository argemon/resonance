## Explicit Non-Goals

- Implementing audio processing in the foundation stage.
- Implementing networking in the foundation stage.
- Implementing a wire protocol in the foundation stage.
- Binding the core to any game engine.
- Shipping a monolithic all-in-one runtime.
- Optimizing for a single engine at the expense of the overall ecosystem.

## Why They Matter

Non-goals keep the foundation disciplined.
They help the project remain focused on architecture, shared language, and long-term maintainability.
