## Core Principles

- Engine agnostic by default.
- Modular boundaries over monolithic convenience.
- Simple concepts over speculative complexity.
- Documentation first for structural decisions.
- Performance awareness without premature optimization.
- Longevity over short-term shortcuts.

## Design Implications

These principles mean the project should avoid hidden coupling, avoid unnecessary abstractions, and keep the path from concept to integration straightforward.
