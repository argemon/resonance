## Purpose

Governance defines how Resonance evolves without losing architectural coherence.
The goal is to keep decision making lightweight, transparent, and durable.

## Decision Making

- Architectural decisions should be recorded when they affect future compatibility, layering, or public vocabulary.
- Small, local changes may proceed through normal review.
- Larger changes should include a design note or ADR before implementation.

## Maintainers

Maintainers are responsible for repository health, review quality, release integrity, and policy enforcement.
As the project grows, the maintainer model may evolve into a broader technical steering structure.

## ADRs

Architecture Decision Records live in `decisions/`.
They document the rationale behind important choices and provide a durable reference for future contributors.

## Release Authority

Only maintainers or designated release managers should cut official releases or publish release notes.
